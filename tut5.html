Pneumonia Claims Six More Lives in Punjab, Pakistan.

https://arynews.tv/pneumonia-c....

Six more children have died from pneumonia in Punjab, bringing the total number of deaths to 347. The Punjab Health Department reported 475 new cases of pneumonia, with 175 cases in Lahore alone. Pneumonia is an infection in the lungs, often caused by viruses, and is more common in children aged five and younger. Health authorities are urging public awareness and preventive measures to control the spread of the infection.
